## Seed Notes (from initial brief)

- Chthonic void entity — cross between a kitsune spirit, a cnidarian, and an octopus
- Purple/blue tones, yellow/gold eyes
- Fox-eared blob with an array of tails and tentacle-legs — chibi anime fox-shaped
- Form unravels during movement, reforms into fox shape at rest
- Matt/shiny parts for uncanny reflectivity; possibly watercolour or scratchy edges
- At least one deliberate distortion marking her as not entirely natural
- St. Elmo's fire effect surrounds her
- Can adhere to screen edges, perch on window corners, drape across furniture
- Name: Shoelace (Lacey for short)
